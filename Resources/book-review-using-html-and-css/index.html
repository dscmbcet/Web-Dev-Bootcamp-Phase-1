A Book Review


The Alchemist

By Paulo Coeleho


"If you truly have a desire for something and want to conquer it,
the whole universe comes together and helps you to achieve it."

Brief

This book is about a young boy who seemingly had a tough life growing up in spain. He sets off to seek his destiny to Egypt to obtain treasure.
He sells some of his sheep. Mid-way in his journey he stumbles upon a king who gives him 2 precious stones.
This story is all about omens and destinies. He after a while realizes he needs more money to go to egypt so he serves as an assistant to a crystal merchant.
After a year or so, he gains enough money and sets off. He makes friends with an englishman and soon their caravan reaches an oasis.
There he meets the love of his life,Fathima
Then by some luck he meets an alchemist who soon accompanies him in th rest of the journey.
Finally the boy reaches the pyramids but he gets beaten up there and in the end he goes away with a gold-plate,
Fathima,and a whole lot of knowledge he had acquired throughoyt the journey.
